I had the opportunity to work with Nifty, a startup focused on promoting creativity in the community. Nifty's goal was to create a platform that gathered creative events in Denmark, where users could buy event tickets, explore the benefits of crafting, read interesting blogs, and learn about different crafts.

Initially, Nifty relied heavily on social media, and their website was somewhat neglected. The website had design inconsistencies and didn't align with the company's vision and branding.

We started by researching Nifty's goals thoroughly. Then, we began the website redesign process to make it more visually appealing and user-friendly. To maintain a consistent look and feel, we established a design system as our guide.

For the design work, we used Figma to refine our ideas. Once the design was ready, we smoothly transitioned it into WordPress to bring our creative vision to life on the website. During our work with WordPress, we employed plugins, improved SEO, and incorporated custom code to boost the website's performance.

This experience not only helped Nifty but also allowed me to grow in UX/UI design and front-end development.

Skills:

  • User-Friendly Design: Creating interfaces that are easy for users to navigate and understand.
  • Design Consistency: Establishing a system to ensure a cohesive visual identity.
  • Figma Usage: Using Figma for collaborative design work.
  • WordPress Integration: Implementing our designs on the website with WordPress.
  • Collaborative Group Work: Gaining valuable experience in working effectively within a team, enhancing my ability to communicate and collaborate with colleagues to achieve project goals.
